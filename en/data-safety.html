Types of Data Processed

The app does not directly collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or account information. Third-party services used by the app may process limited data such as advertising ID, device identifiers, app interactions, crash reports, and purchase/subscription information.

Purposes

Data may be used for advertising, subscription management, app functionality, analytics, troubleshooting, security, and performance improvement.

Data Sharing

Data may be shared with Google AdMob, Firebase Analytics, Firebase Crashlytics, and RevenueCat. The developer does not collect user data through a separate proprietary server.

Data Security

Data is processed through the secure connections and security standards of the relevant third-party service providers. User-created content is stored locally on the device.

Data Deletion

Because the app does not have user accounts, there is no account-based deletion system. Uninstalling the app may remove locally stored app data from the device. Data processed by third-party services is subject to the privacy controls of those providers.
